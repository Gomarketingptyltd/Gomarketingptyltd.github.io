Our Interior Design Service

What is residential interior design?

Residential interior design is the process of creating functional, aesthetically pleasing, and comfortable living spaces within a home. The goal of residential interior design is to enhance the quality of life and create a personalized living environment that meets the unique needs and tastes of the homeowner.

A residential interior designer typically starts by meeting with the homeowner to discuss their design goals, budget, and timeline. They then conduct a comprehensive assessment of the existing space, taking into account factors such as lighting, traffic flow, and storage requirements.

Based on this assessment, the designer will create a custom design plan that incorporates furniture placement, colour schemes, materials and finishes, lighting, and accessories. The designer may also provide recommendations for renovations or remodelling to create a cohesive, functional living space.

Residential interior design often involves close collaboration between the homeowner and the designer, as well as other professionals such as architects, builders, and craftsmen. The designer's job is to help bring the homeowner's vision to life while also taking into account practical considerations such as budget, safety, and building codes.

The end result of a residential interior design project should be a living space that is both beautiful and functional, and that reflects the homeowner's personal style and tastes.

What is commercial interior design?

Commercial interior design is the process of creating functional and aesthetically pleasing spaces within commercial buildings, such as offices, restaurants, hotels, retail stores, and other types of businesses. The goal of commercial interior design is to enhance the overall user experience, promote productivity and comfort, and align with the brand identity and business goals of the organization.

Commercial interior designers work with business owners, building managers, and other stakeholders to create design plans that take into account factors such as the layout and flow of the space, lighting, acoustics, accessibility, and ergonomics. They also consider the organization's brand identity, budget, and timeline when making design decisions.

The design process typically begins with a comprehensive assessment of the existing space and an analysis of the business's specific needs and goals. The designer will then create a custom design plan that incorporates furniture placement, colour schemes, materials and finishes, lighting, and accessories. They may also provide recommendations for renovations or remodelling to create a cohesive and functional workspace.

Commercial interior design projects often involve close collaboration with other professionals such as architects, builders, engineers, and specialized contractors. The designer's job is to ensure that the design plan is practical, efficient, and safe, while also aligning with the business's overall brand and aesthetic vision.

The end result of a commercial interior design project should be a functional and attractive workspace that supports the business's operations and enhances the overall user experience for employees, customers, and visitors.

What is shop interior design?

Shop interior design involves creating an attractive and functional environment within retail spaces, such as boutiques, department stores, and specialty shops. The goal of shop interior design is to create a visually appealing space that enhances the shopping experience and supports the sales and marketing objectives of the business.

Shop interior designers work closely with business owners and other stakeholders to understand the specific goals and requirements of the business. They consider factors such as the target audience, brand identity, product mix, and budget when creating a design plan.

The design process typically begins with a comprehensive assessment of the existing space and an analysis of the business's specific needs and goals. The designer will then create a custom design plan that incorporates elements such as lighting, colour schemes, materials and finishes, furniture placement, and display systems.

Shop interior design projects often involve close collaboration with other professionals such as architects, builders, engineers, and specialized contractors. The designer's job is to ensure that the design plan is practical, efficient, and safe, while also aligning with the business's overall brand and aesthetic vision.

The end result of a shop interior design project should be an inviting and appealing space that supports the sales and marketing objectives of the business and enhances the shopping experience for customers. By creating an environment that is both functional and aesthetically pleasing, shop interior design can play a key role in attracting and retaining customers and boosting sales.
